About Us

At the Krusty Krab, we are dedicated to delivering the freshest and most flavorful meals under the sea, made with the finest ingredients and a sprinkle of love. Founded by the crafty and enterprising Mr. Krabs, the Krusty Krab has been a cherished institution in Bikini Bottom for years, renowned for our signature Krabby Patty. More than just a restaurant, the Krusty Krab is a hub for friends and families to gather, share stories, and enjoy great food together. Our menu features unique offerings that delight both young and old, from the classic Krabby Patty to the colorful Pretty Patties that bring a smile to every face.
